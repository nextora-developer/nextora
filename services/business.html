What We Help You With

Handling business operations in Malaysia can be complex. We support you with end-to-end business services and compliance solutions so you stay organised, legal and ready for growth.

  • Accounting & bookkeeping — keep your numbers clean and updated.
  • Tax filing (Malaysia LHDN) — proper documentation and submission on time.
  • Business licensing, CIDB & compliance — guidance for applications and renewals.
  • Business consultation & digital transformation — improve workflow and structure.
  • HR & documentation support — letters, forms, policies and records.

Our goal is simple: make your business compliant, organised and ready to scale.

Who We Usually Support

BRIF works closely with SMEs, contractors and growing businesses that need structure, compliance and better control over their operations.

SMEs & Family Businesses

For businesses that want proper accounts, tax and documentation — but don’t have an in-house team.

Contractors & CIDB Projects

Help with licensing, CIDB matters and compliance documents needed for tenders and projects.

Growing & Digitalising Companies

For companies ready to move to more structured operations with digital systems and clear workflow.
